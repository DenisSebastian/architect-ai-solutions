Satellite imagery gives us an unprecedented window into how our planet changes over time. In this post, I walk through the end-to-end pipeline I built for detecting land cover transitions across the Chilean Central Valley using multi-temporal Sentinel-2 imagery and a U-Net architecture.

## The Problem

The Chilean Central Valley is one of South America's most productive agricultural regions — and one of its most rapidly changing. Urban expansion, agricultural intensification, and drought-driven land abandonment are reshaping the landscape at rates that traditional field surveys cannot track.
