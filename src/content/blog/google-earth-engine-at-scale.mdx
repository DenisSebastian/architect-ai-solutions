# Google Earth Engine at Scale: Lessons from 50,000 km²

Google Earth Engine is the most powerful tool in a remote sensing analyst's toolbox — and also the one most likely to silently give you wrong answers if you're not careful.

## The Scale Problem

When your analysis covers an entire country, naive approaches break down. A loop that works on 100 tiles fails silently on 10,000. Memory limits kick in. Export tasks queue for hours.

## Key Lessons

### 1. Never trust `getInfo()` in production
### 2. Always export to Cloud Storage, not Drive
### 3. Use `reduceRegions()` not `reduceRegion()` on loops
### 4. Composite before classifying
